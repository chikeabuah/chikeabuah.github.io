Bio

Chiké Abuah is a computer scientist, specializing in data privacy, cybersecurity, and program analysis. He received his PhD in computer science at the University of Vermont in 2021. Currently he researches and implements program analysis tools which help programmers build reliable software. Chiké received his BA from Grinnell College, and MS from the University of Massachusetts.
